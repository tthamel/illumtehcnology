Powering the Evolution of Light

Illum Technologies' line of drivers are quite simply the most elegant solution for any lighting application. Our drivers are fully flash programmable via our easy to use programming software and handheld tool. With our driver you will only need one SKU for an entire product line. The ability to achieve this lies in our ability to program either constant current or constant voltage parameters into our drivers. Our drivers also offer the most control available in this size and price range when compared against any other driver on the market. Our drivers can be programmed for 0-10v, 0-5v, PWM, TRIAC, and ELV dimming. Our drivers also offer the ability to come with DMX 512 control as an option in the same package. One driver, one SKU, multiple possibilities.
